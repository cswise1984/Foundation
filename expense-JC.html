Exercise: What is Job Costing?

Job costing can be a tricky thing to define. As daily users of Foundation, you take for granted that when clients need help with job costing, they typically need help understanding how it works in Foundation, not what it is as a concept.

Exercise: Using Something Familiar to Explain Job Costing

Job costing is a lot easier to understand and explain when using an example that everyone has done, such as baking a cake. This familiarity also makes it easier to start explaining how Foundation job costs transactions.
