CONTINUING DEVELOPMENT

Continuing Development: Expense Track

Welcome to your continuing development program! We’re excited to be offering this program as we know how challenging it can be to learn Foundation on your own.

Expense Track Goals

The objective of this program is to provide participants with a better understanding of how Foundation tracks expenses throughout the system. The following things will be covered in this program:

Base Modules: PS, AP, PR, GL, JC Additional Modules: EQ, FA, IN, SD
- Best practices for expense tracking - Explain the module's purpose
- Showing how expenses flow through the base modules - Best practices for module setup and expense tracking
- Best practices for processing daily transactions
- Showing how expenses flow through the additional and base modules

Classroom Guidelines

We want to lay out our expectations for the class so that everyone can get the most out of it.

  1. Make the Class Time Count: You’ll only be meeting as a group once a week for two hours so it’s important to make the most of this class time. Please make sure you’re in the classroom and ready to learn at 8:00 AM.
  2. This Isn’t a Lecture: These programs are designed to be interactive. Smaller class sizes give you the opportunity to ask questions and participate in discussions and your facilitator is expecting to hear your knowledge and experiences on the topic.
  3. Learning Outside the Classroom: You’ll get the most out of this program if you apply what you’re learning when helping clients and then bring those experiences back into the classroom the following week.
